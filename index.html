Miami Spice

Based in South Florida, our cuisine focuses on South Asian / Arabian / Mediterranean food. We hope to bring the flavors of our home to the beautiful city of Miami and introduce more people to the delicacies of the Asian continent.

Chicken Tikka

6 pieces of boneless chicken baked using skewers on a brazier over charcoal. Marinated in Indian spices and dahi yogurt. Comes with garlic naan.

Biryani

Comes with choice of chicken or goat. Spiced mix of meat and rice, cooked over an open fire in a leather pot. Comes with yogurt or choice of dressing on side.

Samosas with Dal Soup

4 piece samosas filled with minced meat, vegetables, and potatoes. Comes with Dal soup filled with lentils, vegetables, leafy greens, and spices/herbs.
